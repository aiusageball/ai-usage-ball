FAQ

Good questions

How does it read my usage?

It reads from the sessions already on your Mac (the same login your CLI / browser uses) and queries each provider's own usage endpoint locally. Your credentials never leave your machine and are never sent to us.

Which tools are supported?

Claude (Anthropic), Codex / ChatGPT (OpenAI) and Antigravity. Each orb shows remaining quota and the time until it resets.

Is it a subscription?

No. It's a one-time A$9.99. No account, no recurring charge.

What's the free trial?

30 days, fully featured, no card required. After that you can unlock it permanently with a one-time payment or it gently locks.

Does it work on Apple Watch?

The Apple Watch companion is currently in development. The Mac app already shows the same live orbs and countdowns — watch support is coming in a future update.
